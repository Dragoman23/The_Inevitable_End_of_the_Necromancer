This is a text-based adventure that I created about a warlock who was betrayed by his teammates and his kingdom. He gets consumed by his rage and turns into a necromancer. Upon returning to the guild, he can tell the truth or lie to the guild master. If he lies, he must fake a life as a warlock. If he tells the truth, he must run away and join the Dark Guild. The Dark Guild has its own set of challenges and options for you to experience. The life of this necromancer is always short and sweet. I use the time library and the time. The sleep() function adds occasional breaks in between the text.
